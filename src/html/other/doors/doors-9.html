home control

INFINITI systems are compatible with comprehensive smart home solutions that are controlled via a phone app or with the help of a voice assistant. With the TaHoma® Switch control panel, you get full control over the entire home ecosystem, which may also include e.g. window coverings and lighting. Scenarios created easily and activated with one button significantly increase the comfort of household members.
