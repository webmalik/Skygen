Aluminum Pergolas

The backyard terrace is a place where social and family life is vibrant. The pergolas we produce offer several square feet of additional space for spending free time outside, regardless of the weather conditions. Durable aluminum construction and proven components will allow you to enjoy your home terrace for many years.

The Pergola is an extension of the living room to the garden part. The structure consists of a stable aluminum frame and an opening roof, thanks to which the amount of light on the terrace can be adjusted. Innovative solution as rain, wind and temperature sensor give you extra opportunities to access the space regardless of weather conditions, while not requiring any moves.

  1. max width 13' 1"
  2. max height 9' 10"

Lighting & sensors Additional options as LED lighting around the perimeter of the structure or automatic control systems by rain, wind and temperature sensors gives you more calm and enjoyment during using this space.
Movable lamellae Depending on the weather and yours preference roof can be open to the sun or close if need more shadow or rain is coming. And all of this can be done just by click the button or automatically by sensors.
Drainage system Proper water drains from the roof is ensured by special drainage which runs from the upper part through a tunnel in the legs of the pergola as far as the outlet in their lower parts.
