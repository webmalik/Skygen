ESPERIA LIFE 92

Esperia Life 92 is the widest profile of the entire series. Suitable for many applications: single or double sash windows and balcony windows. Thanks to the larger glazing area and the lack of an internal glazing bead, the windows achieve high thermal and acoustic parameters.

  1. Construction depth [mm] 92
  2. Glazing with packages [mm] 24-58
  3. Layers of glued wood 4
  4. Layers of varnish 4
  5. Gaskets 3
  6. Aluminium spacer 0,8
  7. Ultimate spacer
