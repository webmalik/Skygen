FITTINGS

Multi-point locking hardware in the RC1 and RC2 security class. When choosing windows, it is worth paying attention to the class of anti-burglary protection.

RC1 and RC2 are burglary resistance classes that have been defined in the European standard PN-EN 1627: 2012. The markings tell you how effective the window technique is for a burglar. It is determined based on very strict criteria. RC2 windows should be installed where there is a risk of burglary. They will work well on high and low ground floors - places to which a thief can get a very easy access to, as well as at balcony and terrace doors. RC1 windows, in turn, have standard security.
