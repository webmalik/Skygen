swisspacer ultimate spaser frames

In addition to the standard frame made of aluminium, we also offer one of the best warm spacers available in the world.

With the Swisspacer Ultimate spacer, the 4 factor is only 0.03 W / m?K. The lower value of this coefficient not only reduces the possibility of water vapour condensation on the edges of the glass, but also allows for a significant reduction of the thermal transmittance coefficient for the entire window.

The frames are available in several colours from the RAL palette.
